What are the health benefits of eating green vegetables?

Health Benefits of Green Veggies You’ve probably heard most of your life how important it is to eat your vegetables. The suggestion “eat the rainbow” is a great way to vary your nutrient intake, as different colors of vegetables contain different nutrients.
